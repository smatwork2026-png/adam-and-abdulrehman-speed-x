OUR STORY

SpeedX Bikes was born from a simple passion - the love of riding. What started as a small garage operation in 2011 has grown into one of the region's premier motorcycle showrooms.

We believe every rider deserves access to world-class machines and expert care. That's why we've dedicated ourselves to curating the finest motorcycles from around the globe and providing service that matches the quality of our bikes.

Our team of certified technicians and motorcycle enthusiasts ensures that whether you're buying your first bike or adding to your collection, you'll receive the guidance and support you deserve.

OUR JOURNEY

15 years of passion, growth, and serving the riding community.

2011

The Beginning

SpeedX opens as a small service shop with just 3 employees and a dream.

2015

Showroom Expansion

Moved to our current 10,000 sq ft facility with full showroom and service center.

2018

Premium Partnerships

Became authorized dealers for Kawasaki, Ducati, BMW, and Harley-Davidson.

2021

Electric Revolution

Launched our electric motorcycle division, embracing the future of riding.

2026

Today & Beyond

500+ bikes sold annually, 15 expert staff, and counting. The journey continues.
